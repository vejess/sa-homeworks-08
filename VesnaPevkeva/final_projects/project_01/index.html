about us

We are a future-facing brand strategy and innovation agency based in Sofia, Bulgaria.

We are passionate about uncovering, capturing and understanding insights in order to help our clients create successful and sustainable strategies that withstand the test of time.

We are consumer-facing and not consumer-led brand strategists: we help create brands and products that emotionally connect with consumers of global businesses.

We challenge existing ideas and believe in disruptive patterns of thinking. We go beyond brainstorming towards a valuable and demanding debate.
